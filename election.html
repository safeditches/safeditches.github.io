About the Candidates we Support Running for NID Board of Directors

Elect Bruce Herring to NID Division 2

“For over a dozen years I made my living on rivers. In doing so one learns a great deal about the operations and misconceptions of dams and reservoirs. I have closely followed water issues in the Western U.S. ever since. I’m a worker. You can count on me to do my homework, listen to your concerns, then roll up my sleeves and make it happen.” -Bruce Herring

Elect Laura Peters to NID Division 4

Experience matters when it comes to water in California today. The Nevada Irrigation District needs Laura Peters to help navigate current and future issues. Her 26 years of experience as a Civil Engineer in the field of water resources, in the public and private sectors, make her uniquely qualified to represent Division 4.

From Ritchie Marks...

Friends:
In November there will be a very important election for NID Division 2 for Director. If you live in Division 2 or have friends in our division please feel free to send them this message. To me Bruce Herring is the best candidate for this important election. When elected he will help change the culture at NID to being a transparent and physically responsible water agency.

John Drew dropped out and Chris Bierwagen is now running. I love Chris and Ernie, his father and grandfather were key agricultural folks that helped convert our ole mining ditches to a water delivery system for homes and farms of Nevada and Placer Counties. I love our NID's Chicago Park Ditch that brings water to me and my neighbors. Chris filed for candidacy in the last day and I am sure he will follow in his grandfather and fathers footsteps. Ernie's name is on the plaque overlooking Rollings Dam upon completion in 1964. This is definitely not 1964 and days of Damming all the Rivers is over.

We will need two other directors to flip the boards direction.
Ricki Heck is running unopposed in District 1 who is a SYRCL Board Member and will be a great Director.

Laura Peters is also running in Division 4 and is a great candidate with lots of experience. Please view her website.

We will need all 3 to flip the NID board.

Please do what you can.
